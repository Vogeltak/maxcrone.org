Man walks past a Buddhist temple
May 29, 2024

At the quiet and almost deserted temple grounds of this Buddhist temple and cemetery in Kyoto, this man walked past while drying his forehead with a handkerchief. It was elongated-shadows late already, and I needed to rush to still see the sunset at the summit of Inariyama.
