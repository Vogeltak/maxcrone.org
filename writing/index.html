Micro-blog

For very short thoughts I wanted to publish somewhere. Most recent snippet:

Nina Menkes's Brainwashed: sex-camera-power does it all. It serves both as a masterfully articulated indictment of the patriarchal and propagandist status quo and as an inspiring anthology of post-patriarchal cinema. Nina incites within us a yearning for ambiguous excitement and significance far beyond the male gaze. Here's to the hope that we can transcend the shallow male-centricity of cinema and actually explore the richness of humanity.
