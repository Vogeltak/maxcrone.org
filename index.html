Max Crone

security, internet, fair world

I am a student in the Security and Cloud Computing Erasmus Mundus Joint Master programme (SECCLO), for which I am awarded the HAIC scholarship.

My academic interests are in the security and privacy of computer networks and systems.

Previously I studied at the Eindhoven University of Technology, where I received my BSc Computer Science and Engineering with a Cum Laude distinction. I was part of the Honors Society, where I focused on competitive programming and problem solving.

In 2016 I was awarded the Socrates International Honour Society recognition of excellence. I am also a member of the IEEE, Computer Society and Internet Society organizations.
