Abstract

I am a Computer Science student at the TU/e (Eindhoven University of Technology) with interests in security and privacy of computer networks. Recently I was admitted to the Erasmus Mundus Joint Master's programme SECCLO (Security and Cloud Computing), thus I will be furthering my development in Finland soon. I was awarded the HAIC scholarship as partial funding for SECCLO.

At the TU/e I have been part of the Honors Society, focusing on competitive programming and problem solving. In 2016 I was awarded the Socrates International Honour Society recognition of excellence. I am also a member of the IEEE, Computer Society and Internet Society organizations.
